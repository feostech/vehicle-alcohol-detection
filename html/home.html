Ethanol Detection &
Monitoring
Site

This website serves as a crucial tool for detecting ethanol levels in various environments,
With advanced sensor technology, It continuously monitors ethanol levels.
When elevated levels of ethanol are detected,such as in the breath of a driver,
the system triggers an immediate alert. This alert is designed to ensure driver safety and
compliance with road rules and regulations.the website aims to prevent potential accidents
caused by impaired driving due to alcohol consumption. The primary goal is to enhance
road safety for drivers, passengers, and pedestrians alike. In this website the Authorized users only
able to access the Monitoring Dashboard.
